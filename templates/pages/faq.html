General Questions

GeniTrip AI is an advanced travel planning platform that uses artificial intelligence to create personalized travel itineraries. Our system analyzes your preferences, budget, and travel dates to generate comprehensive trip plans including attractions, accommodations, transportation options, and cost estimates.

Yes, the basic features of GeniTrip AI are completely free to use. You can generate itineraries, view travel recommendations, and download PDF versions of your trip plans at no cost. We may introduce premium features in the future, but our core services will always remain free.

No, you don't need to create an account to use our basic services. You can generate and download itineraries without signing up. However, creating an account allows you to save your itineraries, receive personalized recommendations based on your travel history, and access additional features.

Itinerary Planning

GeniTrip AI uses real-time data from mapping services to calculate travel times and distances. While we strive for accuracy, factors like traffic conditions, weather, and road construction can affect actual travel times. We recommend allowing some buffer time in your schedule, especially for important connections.

Currently, GeniTrip AI generates complete itineraries based on your inputs. While direct customization within the platform is not yet available, you can download the PDF and modify it according to your preferences. We're working on adding customization features in future updates to allow you to add, remove, or rearrange activities directly on our platform.

Our AI analyzes thousands of attractions and activities based on popularity, user ratings, seasonal appropriateness, and proximity to your accommodation. The system prioritizes must-see attractions while also including some lesser-known gems to provide a balanced experience. The recommendations are tailored to create a logical flow throughout your day, minimizing travel time between locations.
